Game Controls

W - Player moves Forward

A - Player moves Left

S - Player moves Backwards

D - Player moves Right

R - Reload Gun

E - Switch Gun

How to play game?

Defeat Enemies on each Level.

Once each enemy is killed you can pass to the next level.

Collect Coins to aim for a high score.

Try not to die to Enemy attacks and Bomb explosions.
